How SwiftPay works?

Its convenient,faster and cheaper!

Create your Wallet

Using your basic details create a wallet for free.

Details

Fill in the information about the amount to be sent and the recipient

Select Currency

Select your transaction currency from the given options.This currency is converted into cryptocurrency called Bolt

Pay

Pay to your recipient in the form of Bolts

Receive

SwiftPay converts your Bolts to cash, and sends cash to recipient

Get Notified

Once the transaction is completed, the sender and receiver get notified.
